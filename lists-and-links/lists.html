Business Major Definitions

Return on Investment
Shows how much you gained or lost on a business investment relative to how much you spent on it.
Cash Flow
The movement of money in and out of your business.
Profit Margin
How much profit you keep relative to total sales.
Owner's Equity
The owner's portion of a business' assets.
Net Profit
Represents total revenues less total expenses.
